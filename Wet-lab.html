Wet lab services

Our wet-lab services cover experimental research in molecular biology, cell biology, and biochemistry. We handle sample preparation, cell culture, enzyme assays, nucleic acid/protein extraction, and quality control, providing reliable data to support your scientific investigations.

  1. Home
  2. Wet-lab Services

Molecular Cloning & Gene Expression

We perform precise gene cloning, vector design, and expression analysis, enabling functional studies and protein production for research and biotechnological applications.




PCR & qPCR Analysis

Our team conducts high-quality PCR and quantitative PCR experiments to amplify, detect, and quantify DNA/RNA sequences accurately for diagnostics, gene expression, and validation studies.




Protein Expression & Purification

We produce and purify recombinant proteins using bacterial, yeast, or mammalian expression systems, providing material for functional assays, structural studies, or antibody generation.

Other Services

Cell Culture & Maintenance

We maintain and handle mammalian or microbial cell lines, supporting experiments like transfections, drug testing, and phenotypic studies.

Enzyme Assays & Activity Studies

We perform enzyme activity measurements and biochemical assays to characterize proteins, monitor metabolic pathways, or validate experimental results.

Sample Preparation & Quality Control

We provide nucleic acid/protein extraction, purification, and quality assessment, ensuring samples are ready for downstream analysis and experiments.
